About us

The purpose of this website is to provide information (brief summary and scorecards) to all NFL and NBA fans. This help keep the enthusiasts updated on their favourite teams and players, making them feel a part of these sports. The vision is to encourage all fans, regardless of age, background or ability to come together as a community and cheer for their favourite team/player.
